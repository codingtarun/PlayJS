# PlayJS — Learning & Career Plan

A **12-month** plan (recalibrated for ~2 hours/day) to transition from Laravel/PHP to a full-stack **Node + React + Tailwind** developer targeting a **25 LPA** product-based company by **2027-04-30**, with PlayJS published as a public skills portfolio at https://codingtarun.github.io/PlayJS/.

> **Why 12 months and not 6?** At 2 h/day = ~14 h/week, the realistic budget is ~700 study hours, vs the ~900 the original 6-month plan assumed (5 h/day). 25 LPA at product cos requires DSA + system design + production projects on top of framework knowledge — none of those are skippable. See [ROADMAP.md § "Why 12 months"](ROADMAP.md#why-12-months-not-6) for the two-option breakdown.

## Documents

1. **[ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md)** — Week-by-week, 26-week plan (May → Nov 2026). Covers JS deep-dive, React, Node, DSA, system design, projects, and interview-prep cadence.
2. **[SKILL-GAPS.md](SKILL-GAPS.md)** — Senior-engineer / recruiter perspective: what a 25 LPA candidate looks like in 2026, what your Laravel background gives you for free, and the specific gaps interviewers will probe.
3. **[PORTFOLIO-APP-IDEAS.md](PORTFOLIO-APP-IDEAS.md)** — Unique apps you can host on GitHub Pages (static-only constraint), with a recommended "anchor" concept that turns PlayJS itself into a living portfolio.

## How to use this folder

- **Treat ROADMAP.md as the primary source of truth.** Tick off weeks as you go. Don't skip DSA weeks — they are the gating factor for product-co interviews.
- **Re-read SKILL-GAPS.md every 4 weeks.** It's calibrated to what interviewers actually look for, not what tutorials teach.
- **Pick one anchor project from PORTFOLIO-APP-IDEAS.md by end of Week 4.** Ship something deployable by Week 8 — momentum compounds.
- **Keep this folder under version control on master.** Day-N branches are for exercises; the plan stays on master.

## Honest reality check

25 LPA at Indian product-based companies (Razorpay, CRED, PhonePe, Swiggy, Flipkart, Postman, Atlassian, Microsoft India, etc.) typically maps to **SDE-2 / 3–5 YOE** roles. Frameworks alone won't get you there — you need:

1. Strong **DSA** (200–300 LeetCode problems, can solve mediums in 25 min)
2. **System design** fundamentals (HLD for at least 8–10 classic problems)
3. **Two to three production-quality projects** with real users or open-source traction
4. **Deep JS + React internals** knowledge (not just usage)

Your Laravel experience is a real asset — it counts as backend YOE. The path is realistic at **2 hours/day for 12 months**, with weekend project blocks of 3–4 hours. See [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md) for the daily/weekly breakdown and the catch-up rules if you fall behind.
